Method:Wavelet Transform


As stated above, the two dimensional wavelet transform is simply row wavelet transform followed by a column wavelet transform. It could have been done in reverse order. However, the result would be the same. Just of note, this class lacks for the moment an inverse transform function. Not that such a thing does not exist mathematically. It simply was not implemented at the time of this document.
