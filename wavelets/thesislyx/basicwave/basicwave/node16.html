Join Procedure

The join procedure is rather simple. Produce a result whose size is the size of the two sources combined, and whose elements are those of the two input vectors. Input:         myVector left, right Output:         myVector Result Algorithm         result.deallocate                                    result.allocate(s)                          result[i]=left[i]                          result[i]= right
